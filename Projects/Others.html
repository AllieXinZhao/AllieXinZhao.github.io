HemoGuard

More

Hemo Guard is a game-for-change project. Our team decided to make an educational game - a game about cells vs. viruses.

In the game, the players' objective is to defeat invading creatures by generating different types of immune cells. The immune cells have different prices and unique effects on different invading organisms.

For example, the price of Eosinophil is 1.5 times that of a Lymphocyte, and its attack power on a Parasitic worm egg is 2 times that of Lymphocyte. At the same time, its attack power on common bacteria is 0.5 of Lymphocyte. Therefore, players need some strategy to beat the game, and at the same time, they can learn more about immune cells.

Changeling

More

Changeling is a VR narrative game developed by RIT that will be released at the end of 2022. As one of the first term developers, I worked as a concept artist, 3D modeler, and game designer.

Model production mainly uses Maya for the basic model. The details are supplemented by Zbrush, and the texture is completed using Substance Painter and Adobe Ps.

The game's overall flow is designed before we are in, so I didn't work a lot on game design. I only made some suggestions, such as adding shadow of characters on the ground behind the title to indicate progression.
