Web Games

About this Part

These web-based games are derived from my web development course project. Although they began as academic projects, it is more than that. Since making web games is the easiest way for me to prototype, I decided to spend extra time extending them beyond the requirement to make the game I wanted.

The game is made with pixiJS. The Little garden is a simple garden simulator, while 100Time is a horror puzzle game (please note that there will be some game screens displayed)

The Little graden

The Little garden is a simple garden simulation game in which players can make money by absorbing the energy from the sun through the earn button. And then, click the plant button to plant different flowers (currently, there are only four kinds of Lily, Rose, Winter Jasmine, and Flor Impia).

The appearance of the flower is based on real life, but every single flower will not be exactly the same, such as the growth curve of the stem, the height of the flower, and the color of the petals and stamens.

The most time-consuming part is choosing and designing the types of flowers and displaying them using functions. For example, using bezierCurveTo(), to draw the petal in different shapes.

100 Times

100 Times is a horror puzzle game about a reporter investigating a haunted house and then being trapped in it. The game's objective is easy, find out two keys to open the door and leave. But, is that all? This game has multiple endings.

The player can easily find two keys, but there are actually three keys in this room. The true ending will only be revealed after the player investigates the truth. Figuring out hidden clues buried in the past of this house is the only way to escape this haunted house.

In addition, the title of the game is also a hint. "Times" also represents the number of mouse clicks. Players must pass the game within 100 clicks, otherwise, they will still be trapped inside.
